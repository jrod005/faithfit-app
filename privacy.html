Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

Iron Faith ("we", "the app") is a fitness and faith tracking application. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the controls you have over it.

Data We Collect

Stored locally on your device

By default, all of your data lives on your device only. This includes:

  • Profile (name, age, weight, height, goals)
  • Workout logs, sets, reps, and weights
  • Nutrition entries and meals
  • Body weight history
  • Custom routines and exercises
  • Prayer journal entries
  • Coach chat history
  • App preferences (units, theme, notification settings)

Stored in the cloud (only if you opt in)

If you create an account and enable Cloud Backup, we store a snapshot of your local data on our backend (Supabase) so you can sync across devices and recover from data loss. The data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored encrypted at rest by our backend provider. We do not perform additional end-to-end encryption — Iron Faith staff with backend access could in principle view your stored data. You can disable cloud backup at any time in Settings.

Stored if you use the Social tab

If you sign in to the Social tab, we collect:

  • Email address (for authentication only)
  • Display name and username
  • Profile picture and bio (optional)
  • Posts, photos, and comments you choose to share publicly
  • Friend connections

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your data. Ever.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers.
  • We do not use third-party analytics that track you across the web.
  • We do not access your device's camera, location, or contacts without your explicit action.

Photos & Body Images

Progress photos taken in the app are stored locally on your device by default. If you choose to post a photo to the Social feed, it is uploaded to our backend storage and is visible according to your post settings.

Notifications

Reminders and notifications are scheduled locally on your device using your browser's Notification API. We do not collect or transmit your reminder schedule.

Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is visible in the app at any time.
  • Export: Use Profile → Export Data to download a JSON copy.
  • Delete: Use Profile → Erase All Data. If signed in, you’ll be offered a “Delete cloud too” option that removes your backup and profile from our servers. Your sign-in email remains in the auth system until you contact us to remove it.

Children's Privacy

Iron Faith is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Any changes will appear on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions or requests? Email support@ironfaith.app.
