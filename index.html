Install on your iPhone in 30 seconds

  1. Open this page in Safari (Chrome won’t work for iOS install).
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari (square with up-arrow).
  3. Scroll down and tap “Add to Home Screen”.
  4. Tap Add. The Iron Faith icon will appear on your home screen like a regular app.

On Android: tap the menu (⋮) and select “Install app” or “Add to Home screen.”
On desktop: look for the install icon in the address bar.
